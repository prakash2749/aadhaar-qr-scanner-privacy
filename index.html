Privacy Policy

Aadhaar QR Code Scanner - Offline Verify

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Aadhaar QR Code Scanner - Offline Verify is an independent offline viewer for Aadhaar Secure QR data. This app is not affiliated with UIDAI or the Government of India.

What the app does

The app scans Aadhaar Secure QR codes from the device camera, imported images, or imported PDF files. It decodes the embedded QR payload on the device and attempts to verify the QR signature using bundled UIDAI public certificates.

Data the app accesses

  • Camera access, only when you choose live scanning.
  • Image or PDF files you choose to import for QR reading.
  • Aadhaar QR fields that may include name, date of birth, gender, address, reference ID, and embedded photo.

How data is processed

  • QR decoding happens on-device.
  • No Aadhaar data is uploaded to a server by the app.
  • No analytics, ads, trackers, or third-party data collection SDKs are included in the app.
  • Redacted share only sends the limited summary shown in the app at the time of sharing.

Storage and retention

  • The app is designed to keep scan results in memory for the current session only.
  • The app does not create its own database of Aadhaar details.
  • The app does not intentionally back up Aadhaar data.

Permissions

  • Camera: used only for live QR scanning.

Important limitations

  • Signature verification is an offline cryptographic check and is not UIDAI online authentication.
  • Some older or legacy QR formats may decode without cryptographic verification.
  • Certificate rotation by UIDAI may require app updates for future verification compatibility.
