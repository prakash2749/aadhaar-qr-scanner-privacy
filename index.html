Privacy Policy

Secure Aadhaar QR Scanner

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Secure Aadhaar QR Scanner is an independent third-party utility app that reads and decodes the publicly available Aadhaar Secure QR code.

This application is not affiliated with UIDAI or the Government of India. The app only displays information already embedded inside the Aadhaar Secure QR code for local verification purposes.

What the app does

The app scans Aadhaar Secure QR codes using the device camera or from images and PDF files selected by the user.

The QR code payload is decoded locally on the device and the embedded information is displayed to the user.

The app may attempt to verify the QR digital signature using bundled public verification certificates.

Data the app accesses

  • Camera access when you choose to scan a QR code.
  • Images or PDF files selected by the user for QR scanning.
  • Aadhaar Secure QR fields such as name, date of birth, gender, address, reference ID, and embedded photograph.

How data is processed

  • All QR decoding happens locally on the device.
  • The app does not upload Aadhaar information to any server.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included.
  • No Aadhaar data is collected or transmitted by the app.
  • Shared results only include the visible summary information displayed in the app when the user chooses to share.

Storage and retention

  • Scan results are kept in memory during the active session.
  • The app does not create a permanent database of Aadhaar data.
  • No Aadhaar information is stored on external servers.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used only for scanning QR codes.

Important limitations

  • QR signature verification is a local cryptographic check and is not UIDAI online authentication.
  • Some older QR formats may decode without full signature verification.
  • Certificate updates from UIDAI may require future app updates for verification compatibility.
